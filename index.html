Problem Statement

During organized events or when navigating unfamiliar big spaces, users have a hard time coming to a decision about where to go next. Most often they come to a conclusion on the basis of a time restriction, places they’ve already visited and where their interests lie. Our team aims to answer the question “Where to Next?” with a playful interactive tangible map that allows the user to figure out where to travel to next by folding and manipulating the map until an answer is revealed.
